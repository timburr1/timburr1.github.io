### Don't Forget!
#### Setup:
* Deploy Venomspitter and Prime/Wrecker
* Always take: Cromatosphere Camoflage, Heightened Senses, Metamorphic Flesh
* Acid Blood vs melee teams
* Barriers/Smoke Grenades vs ranged teams

#### Strategy Phase:
* Tunnel 5"
* Synaptic Link turns 1-2
* Gambits?

#### Firefight Phase:
* Heal D3 on activation (Metamorphic Flesh)
* Use Felltalon last?
